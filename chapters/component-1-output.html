Component 1: Output

Design AI systems to deliver impactful output

Core ideas

  • Output is first in the framework because purpose and end-state experience must orient every downstream decision.
  • Avoid the builder’s trap: inventing solutions and searching for problems; anchor on real business problems and user value.
  • Connect organizational initiatives to each AI module’s output: what is valuable, how it is used, and how integration evolves.
  • Component 1 bridges intent and execution across three moves: org-wide design, opportunity funnel, then workflows and agents.
  • Later components address data (2), models (3), observability (4), and the people and platforms that scale the system (5).

Framing (from the component introduction)

  • Everything must be oriented around the purpose of what you want to create.
  • Problems rarely exist in isolation; outputs must make sense inside the larger organizational ecosystem.
