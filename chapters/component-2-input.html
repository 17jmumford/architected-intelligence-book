Component 2 — Input

Power AI with high-quality input data

Core ideas

  • Input is not “data cleaning” alone: it is building a continuous Knowledge Transformation Cycle from raw information to action.
  • Democratization of expertise has two sides: consumption (people use richer systems with AI) and contribution (people master topics faster and give better feedback)—a flywheel of truth when both run together.
  • Chapters 4–6 move through collect (unlock and acquire), connect (architecture to the moment of value), then curate (authority, canon, feedback).

Framing (from the component introduction)

  • AI-first knowledge systems can democratize both access to expertise and contribution to expertise by humans and machines.
